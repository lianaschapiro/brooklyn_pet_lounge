About Us:


Brooklyn Pet Lounge provides daycare and other services for small and medium-sized breeds. Our supervisors are caring pet professionals who understand canine behavior, communication, and the nuances of off-leash group play. We have both indoor and outdoor space. Our play groups are small, and our facility is big! So every pup has plenty of room to exercise and socialize in a safe, fun and oh-so-comfy environment.
